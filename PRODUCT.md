# PRODUCT.md — AI Brand Scale (clone of aibrandscale.io)

## Register

**brand** — this is a single-page brand/marketing landing. The deliverable is the impression itself; design IS the product.

## Users

Bulgarian-speaking adults (18–40), often with prior failed attempts at making money online (dropshipping, Amazon FBA, SMM agency, trading). Skeptical of "online business gurus." Looking for a concrete, low-overhead path to €5K–€20K/month. Mobile-first; high social-media-referral traffic.

## Product purpose

A free 18-minute video that explains an "AI ad system" — a service-based offer where the operator runs AI-generated paid ads for small businesses (€1,500–€5,000/month per client). The landing page captures emails to deliver the video and book a 10-minute consultation.

## Voice

- Direct, plainspoken Bulgarian. Short sentences.
- Anti-guru framing: "I started where you are, no money, no audience, no degree."
- Punctuated by handwritten emphases (Felt Tip Roman) over a tight sans (Alfabet). Restraint is not the voice — committed magenta is.
- No corporate words. No "innovate," "empower," "synergy."

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with hero-metric grids and gradient-text headlines.
- "Online business guru" sites with stacked car-photo testimonials.
- AI-generated dark/purple gradient soup with no commitment.

## Strategic principles

- **Commit to one color, not gradients of it.** A single saturated magenta (`#d438ff`) on near-black tinted magenta. No multi-stop gradients on type, headings, or stat numbers.
- **Brand fidelity beats personal taste.** Alfabet + DM Sans + Felt Tip Roman Bold are the source's choices; keep them.
- **Handwritten as voice, not decoration.** Felt Tip Roman is reserved for short, deliberate emphases (1–3 words: "Бонус:", "да изкарват повече", "01"/"02"/"03"). Never paragraphs.
- **Asymmetry inside sections.** Don't repeat the centered eyebrow + heading + grid template every fold.
- **Verbatim source copy is sacred.** When the live aibrandscale.io says X, it stays X (em dashes included). My-authored copy avoids em dashes.

## Conversion goal

Email + name → "Готово, изпратихме линка" success state. Sticky mobile CTA + hero CTA + final CTA all open the same opt-in modal.
